Our Services

We provide a variety of services which are Assistive Technology,Employment and Home care services, Music and speech therapies,and vision.

Assistive Technology

Assistive technology can overcome barriers through physical modification, vision services, and communication supports, and workplace accommodations. Our state-wide assistive technology specialists equip individuals with adaptive and rehabilitative devices. Tours, device demonstrations, and reuse services are free (donations are welcome).

Employment Services

We provide comprehensive services to help people with disabilities join or rejoin the workforce. Employment services are primarily available to recipients of Tennessee Vocational Rehabilitation Services. You can apply for VR services by contacting a local VR office.

Home Care Services

The STAR Center's in-home care has helped loved ones stay in their homes since 1997. Care aides are dedicated to making every day the best for you and your loved ones. In-home services can be funded by DIDD, CHOICES, ECF CHOICES or the Veterans Administration.

About Us

We help people with disabilities realize their potential. Serving all 95 Counties in Tennessee.In 1988, Chuck and Margaret Doumitt realized the need for disability resources after two of their children were diagnosed with Batten's Disease, thus founding The STAR Center. We serve others in the areas of education, employment, and independence.
