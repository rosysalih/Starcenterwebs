About us page

We help people with disabilities realize their potential.The STAR Center provides services for education, employment, and independence for all ages and needs in West Tennessee and beyond.We will help a child communicate, help a person get their first job, and help the elderly stay in their homes. We are dedicated to helping people with disabilities make their dreams a reality.

Overview

We are a 501(c)(3) non-profit founded in 1988 and provide services to people with disabilities of all ages and needs. Our 33,000 sq. ft. headquarters is located in the city of Jackson, Tennessee between Memphis and Nashville. We have a staff of over 110, many of whom provide services off-site. Our primary service area is the 21 counties of West Tennessee, but we provide Assistive Technology services in all 95 counties in Tennessee. On an annual basis we serve approximately 4,000 individuals with disabilities or other special needs.

Our Mission and Vision

The mission of the STAR Center is to help any person with any disability to realize their potential. It is intended to be spoken aloud in the form of “any person – with any disability – to realize their potential!” The STAR Center’s vision is to realize our own potential so that we may best serve others.

Our Core values

Our Motto is Education, Employment, Independence These are the guiding principles for how we help people with disabilities realize their potential. We believe in the lifelong value of education, the dignity of employment, and the self-esteem one finds in independence. We serve others with the purpose of helping them find individualized success in these areas.

About Us

We help people with disabilities realize their potential. Serving all 95 Counties in Tennessee.In 1988, Chuck and Margaret Doumitt realized the need for disability resources after two of their children were diagnosed with Batten's Disease, thus founding The STAR Center. We serve others in the areas of education, employment, and independence.
